<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Background</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A major enterprise launched a new subscription-based e-commerce business with a fully custom-built system.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>While the architecture allowed flexibility, there was no function bridging product development and marketing from a measurement perspective.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>As a result, even close to launch, the team lacked visibility into:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>which activities were driving revenue</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>which users were likely to retain</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The business was operating without a reliable foundation for decision making.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Objectives</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Integrate the custom EC system with analytics platforms (GA4, Amplitude)</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Define and visualize subscription metrics such as LTV, retention, and churn</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Establish a data-driven operating model for investment and product decisions</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Challenges</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Disconnection between revenue and behavior data</strong>Marketing performance could not be evaluated in terms of actual revenue contribution</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Lack of subscription-oriented KPIs</strong>Metrics were not designed around recurring revenue and lifecycle value</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>No behavior-driven optimization loop</strong>The team lacked visibility into actions influencing retention and churn</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Delayed decision making</strong>Heavy reliance on manual data aggregation slowed down evaluation and execution</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Approach</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Rather than focusing only on tracking implementation,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>we designed a&nbsp;<strong>data structure and operating model that connects user behavior to revenue outcomes</strong>.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Data architecture design</strong>Unified transactional and behavioral data to enable LTV-level analysis</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Product analytics implementation (Amplitude)</strong>Identified behavioral patterns driving retention</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>KPI alignment across teams</strong>Established shared metrics (LTV, retention) across marketing, product, and management</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Outcomes</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Shift to LTV-based investment decisions</strong>Marketing allocation optimized based on long-term revenue, not just acquisition cost</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Identification of high-retention acquisition channels</strong>Channel performance evaluated based on downstream revenue impact</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Faster product iteration</strong>UX improvements prioritized based on behavioral insights tied to retention</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Real-time decision making</strong>Integrated dashboards enabled immediate visibility into business performance</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Core Value</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This project delivered more than a data infrastructure.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>It established a system where&nbsp;<strong>every activity can be evaluated in terms of its impact on revenue and LTV</strong>.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>As a result, the business was able to operate from day one with</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>a consistent, data-driven approach to growth and investment decisions.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Applicable Scenarios</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>New subscription or e-commerce business launches</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Organizations with custom-built systems and fragmented data</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Teams seeking to shift from acquisition-based to LTV-driven decision making</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
