<p>This Policy explains how Consilegy LLC ("we") handles personal information for our website and related services in Japan. It includes disclosures related to Google Tag Manager (GTM) and HubSpot. Overseas regimes are not covered here.</p>

<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Applies to users of our Services in Japan and covers cookies, tags, pixels, and similar technologies.</p>

<li>Newsletter subscriptions and chat: email address and chat content.</li>

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">4. Cookies and Similar Technologies</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>We use strictly necessary cookies and, with your consent, analytics/advertising/functional cookies. Manage or withdraw consent any time via the on-site "Cookie Settings" link.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">4.1 Google Tag Manager (GTM)</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>GTM is a tag manager and does not itself collect personal data.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Tags deployed through GTM may set/read cookies. We integrate GTM with consent management so non-consented categories do not fire.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">4.2 HubSpot</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>We use HubSpot for forms, CRM, email, chat, and baseline analytics.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Depending on interactions (e.g., form submissions, email link clicks), your browsing history may be associated with your contact record where consent applies.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Example HubSpot cookies (names/expiry may change): hubspotutk, __hstc, __hssc, __hssrc, messagesUtk, __cfruid.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
