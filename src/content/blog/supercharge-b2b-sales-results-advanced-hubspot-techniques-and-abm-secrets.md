<h2>1. Lead Segmentation and Scoring</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:image {"linkDestination":"none","className":"size-large"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="/images/media/2025/03/image-21-1024x585.webp" alt=""/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->
<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"color":{"text":"rgb(0, 0, 0)"}}} -->
<p class="has-text-color" style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0)">In B2B sales, the &quot;quality&quot; of prospects (leads) greatly influences the success of business discussions and orders. Information as basic as &quot;they&#039;ve downloaded materials once&quot; is insufficient for determining priorities.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"color":{"text":"rgb(0, 0, 0)"}}} -->
<p class="has-text-color" style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0)">This is where HubSpot&#039;s &quot;segmentation&quot; and &quot;scoring&quot; features become valuable.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"color":{"text":"rgb(0, 0, 0)"}}} -->
<p class="has-text-color" style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0)">Specifically, by analyzing and scoring lead behavior data from multiple angles—such as email open rates, number of website pages viewed, and whitepaper download frequency—you can identify truly engaged prospects.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"color":{"text":"rgb(0, 0, 0)"}}} -->
<p class="has-text-color" style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0)">For example, a lead who has opened multiple emails and repeatedly viewed product pages clearly shows higher potential than one who hasn&#039;t opened any emails.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"color":{"text":"rgb(0, 0, 0)"}}} -->
<p class="has-text-color" style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0)">By setting up weighted scores for different actions and reviewing them regularly, you can prioritize &quot;targets that need immediate approach,&quot; dramatically improving sales efficiency.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:heading {"style":{"color":{"text":"rgb(0, 0, 0)"}}} -->
<h2>2. Personalized Strategies for Key Accounts</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"color":{"text":"rgb(0, 0, 0)"}}} -->
<p class="has-text-color" style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0)">In B2B contexts, it&#039;s not uncommon for a few major customers to support a significant portion of your business. These strategically important accounts require more attentive measures.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"color":{"text":"rgb(0, 0, 0)"}}} -->
<p class="has-text-color" style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0)">Refine your segments using HubSpot and incorporate techniques that create a sense of &quot;special treatment&quot; for major customers, such as individual email distributions or exclusive offers.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"color":{"text":"rgb(0, 0, 0)"}}} -->
<p class="has-text-color" style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0)">For example, if you&#039;re providing services for a specific industry, it&#039;s effective to publish case studies or solution guides addressing that industry&#039;s unique challenges on a landing page and send direct notification emails to target accounts.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"color":{"text":"rgb(0, 0, 0)"}}} -->
<p class="has-text-color" style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0)">What&#039;s crucial is not treating all prospects or existing customers the same way, but rather setting up different personas for each segment to optimize communication. Utilizing HubSpot&#039;s ABM (Account-Based Marketing) features can further strengthen your company-level approach.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:heading {"style":{"color":{"text":"rgb(0, 0, 0)"}}} -->
<h2>3. Strengthening Collaboration with Sales Teams</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->
<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"color":{"text":"rgb(0, 0, 0)"}}} -->
<p class="has-text-color" style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0)">How to transfer lead information accumulated by the marketing team to the sales team is a challenge many companies face.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"color":{"text":"rgb(0, 0, 0)"}}} -->
<p class="has-text-color" style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0)">In such cases, compile behavioral data and scores obtained through HubSpot into easy-to-understand reports for sales, and establish a system for regular sharing.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"color":{"text":"rgb(0, 0, 0)"}}} -->
<p class="has-text-color" style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0)">For instance, setting up dashboards for sales representatives to immediately access, with real-time views of hot lead lists and high-potential prospects, can be very convenient.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"color":{"text":"rgb(0, 0, 0)"}}} -->
<p class="has-text-color" style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0)">Furthermore, establishing a &quot;feedback loop&quot; that returns insights from the sales perspective to marketing is important.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"color":{"text":"rgb(0, 0, 0)"}}} -->
<p class="has-text-color" style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0)">By gathering information from sales representatives about factors influencing successful or unsuccessful deals and communication status with leads, marketing can improve nurturing scenarios and content, enhancing lead quality and nurturing effectiveness.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
<!-- wp:paragraph {"style":{"color":{"text":"rgb(0, 0, 0)"}}} -->
<p class="has-text-color" style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0)">When this mutual collaboration functions properly, the synergy between &quot;marketing and sales&quot; creates a positive cycle that ultimately increases conversion rates and order rates!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
