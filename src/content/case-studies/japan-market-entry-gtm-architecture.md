<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Background</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>A global technology product with strong market share was preparing for launch in Japan.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>While the global creative followed a minimal and abstract approach, decision-making in Japan relies heavily on detailed specifications, reliability, and post-implementation support.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This gap created a risk where marketing efforts would not translate into sales outcomes, as the messaging and structure were not aligned with local decision-making expectations.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Objective</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>To redesign messaging, information architecture, and conversion flow to align with the Japanese decision-making process, while maintaining global brand consistency.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The goal was to establish a GTM foundation that connects marketing activities directly to sales outcomes.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Challenges</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Misalignment with local decision-making structure</strong>: Global messaging did not resonate with Japanese stakeholders or decision-makers</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Insufficient decision-support information</strong>: Lack of detailed information prevented users from progressing in the evaluation process</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Disconnected marketing-to-sales flow</strong>: No structured path from lead generation to sales engagement</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Limitations of translation-based localization</strong>: Content remained at a translation level and did not achieve true market adaptation</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Approach</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Rather than focusing on page design alone,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>we designed a go-to-market architecture aligned with local decision-making and sales processes.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Repositioning for the Japanese market</strong>: Redefined value proposition based on local competitive landscape and decision criteria</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Decision-driven information architecture</strong>: Structured content to support each stage of the evaluation process</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>End-to-end conversion flow design</strong>: Built a seamless transition from marketing touchpoints to sales engagement</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Alignment with global standards</strong>: Maintained brand consistency while introducing the necessary depth of information for Japan</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Results</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>High-quality, sales-ready lead generation</strong>: Leads were better qualified and aligned with sales expectations</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Improved pipeline quality</strong>: Generated opportunities with higher conversion potential</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Balance between brand and performance</strong>: Achieved strong local performance without compromising global brand standards</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Scalable operating model</strong>: Established a reusable framework for future expansion in the Japanese market</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><strong>Core Value</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This project was not a localization or landing page optimization effort.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>It established a structure that connects marketing activities to revenue outcomes in the Japanese market.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>By aligning messaging, information, and conversion flow with local decision-making,</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>the company was able to build a reliable pipeline from the early stage of market entry.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->
